                                    ===========================
                                    Manga Reader Privacy Policy
                                    ===========================

                 The Manga Reader project is committed to protecting your privacy.
This privacy & disclainer statement applies only to the services made available by the Manga Reader application.

----------------------
# Personal information
----------------------
This application does not collect or transmit any user's personally identifiable information. 
No personal information is used, stored, secured or disclosed by services this application works with.

--------------------------------------------------
# Manga Reader needs to access your Picture folder
--------------------------------------------------
The Manga Reader initializes and maintains a folder named "MangaReaderUserSettings" in your "Pictures" folder.
This folder is only used to save your personal settings of this application. 
The personal settings includes backgrounds, bookmarks, application themes, application view mode, recent visit folder show or hide. 
If you uninstall the Manga Reader from your device, this folder will not be removed.
If you re-install the Manga Reader, this folder can be used to restore your application settings.
The Manga Reader does not transmit any of datas in "MangaReaderUserSettings".
The Manga Reader does not use any of your datas in your "Picture" folder other than "MangaReaderUserSettings" folder.

----------------------------------------
# Manga Reader needs Internet Connection
----------------------------------------
The Manga Reader application only use internet for:
- You want to access to website using service supported by the Manga Reader.
- You want to download image data from website using service supported by the Manga Reader.
- Sending application crash report. The error information contains only application information for diagnosing errors 
in the application for the purpose of improving the application and none other.

The Manga Reader do not sell, lease, rent or otherwise disclose any data to third parties.
